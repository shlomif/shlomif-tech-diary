It is a good day

The Text

It is a good day to die.
It is a good day to kill.
It is a good day to heal.
It is a good day to live.

It is a good day to love, know, and conceive.

It is a good day to forego and forgive.

It is a good day to be deceived, to deceive, to believe, and to dis-believe.

It is a good day to perceive, give, and receive.

It is also a good day to rest, reflect, relax, and recreate.

Seize this good day. Make it a great day. Live.
