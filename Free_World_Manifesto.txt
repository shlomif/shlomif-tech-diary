The Free World Manifesto
========================

Preamble:



Sections:

1. A government may not censor or restrict any means of expression
unless they convey cooersion or fraud.

1 (a) Such means of expression include Speech, Song, Poetry, Writing,
Drawing, Photography, Sculpting, Music as well as any digital
represnetations of them, and computer-specific information such as
source code or binary files.

1 (b) The people may establish voluntary systems to censor such
material. However, the government may not back up or support such
systems.

2. The production, consumption and trade of all food, medicine and
narcotics will be unimpeded by government Law.

2 (a) The people or the government may establish voluntary systems to
control the distribution of such materials.
