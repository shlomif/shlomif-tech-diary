2009-02-17: How Ubuntu Has Excluded Me

For a long time, I felt that it was hard to get the point of my "Ubuntu is Dead" post because it just featured a breadcrumbs trail without fully telling the story of the chains of events that I complained above. In this post, I'll try to remedy this, and tell of my encounter with the Ubuntu online world from start to finish.

It is commonly acceptable that when complaining about an organisation, one should first enumerate the facts, and I intend to obey by it. However, along with the facts, I'll explain some of my motivation for doing what I did. After that, I'll enumerate the problems I see with my treatment, and give some suggestions.
