Solving (see also the Q&A page) is a new idea system that aims to provide innovative solutions for managing countries in the 21st century. It combines the best features of Objectivism, Libertarianism and the Open Source World, and offers some improvements of its own. Among other things, it suggests Distributism (= the system prevalent in the Open Source World) as a system of government and also competing leaderships.
